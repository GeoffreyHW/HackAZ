How it works

Enter your current city as well as a list of your personal interests. Then, our genetic algorithm will automatically create you a unique schedule for the day.

This site was created by the Phoenix AZ Hackers at HackAZ 2017.

Technologies used:

  • Our own genetic algorithm implementation for optimizing schedules.
  • Amadeus and Yelp APIs for retrieving restaurants and activities.
  • Google Geocoding API to retrieve city coordinates.
  • Bootstrap/jQueryfor web interactivity.
  • Leaflet.js for map interactions.
